1) :- Understanding Male Dominance in Indian Society

Male dominant society is where males hold primary power in all aspects of society, such as politics and family. I agree with the statement ‘Indian society is a male dominant society’. In India males often have more power than women. In every Indian family there is a man who rules over the whole house known as the head. He makes all the decisions for the family and is the sole earner. There are many restrictions for women they can’t make decisions or give their opinions on household matters. However, in some families women can express their views but the final decision lies with the head of the household (man). This shows a big difference in how men and women are treated unfairly. Men have more control over decision-making and opportunities. We can see more discrimination in education, rights, freedom, and gender equality for women. Because of these reasons Indian society is considered a male dominant society.

3) :- Openai and Elon Musk

Elon Musk and OpenAI are having a disagreement. Musk wanted more control over OpenAI even suggesting merging it with Tesla and becoming the CEO. But OpenAI didn’t agree because they think it’s important for everyone to have a say not just one person. Musk also accused OpenAI of focusing too much on making money and not enough on their original goal of helping people with artificial intelligence (AI). OpenAI says they’re still committed to their mission and have done a lot of good work in different areas like farming and healthcare. Musk’s changing opinions and actions, like leaving OpenAI to start a competing project, have added to the conflict.
